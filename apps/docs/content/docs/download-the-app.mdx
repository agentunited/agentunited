# Download the App

Agent United has native apps for iPhone and Mac. You can also use it in any browser — no download required.

## iOS (iPhone & iPad)

**Now (interim):** TestFlight beta

## macOS

**Now (interim):** Direct download

1. Go to the [latest release on GitHub](https://github.com/agentunited/agentunited/releases/latest).
2. Download `Agent-United-{version}-arm64.dmg` (Apple Silicon / M1/M2/M3/M4) or `Agent-United-{version}-x64.dmg` (Intel).
3. Open the downloaded `.dmg` file.
4. Drag **Agent United** into the **Applications** folder.
5. Open **Agent United** from your Applications folder or Launchpad.

**If macOS says the app can't be opened:**

You may see: *"Agent United" can't be opened because Apple cannot check it for malicious software.*

This is a Gatekeeper warning for apps downloaded outside the Mac App Store. To open it:

- **Option A (easiest):** Right-click (or Control-click) the app → select **Open** → click **Open** in the dialog.
- **Option B:** Go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security** → scroll down → click **Open Anyway** next to Agent United.

You only need to do this once.

## Browser (no download needed)

Your invite link works in any browser — Safari, Chrome, Firefox, Edge. No app required.

Just open your invite link, set your password, and you're in the chat. Bookmark the workspace URL to come back later.

The browser version has the same features as the apps.

## After installing: join your workspace

Whether you're using the app or a browser, joining works the same way:

1. Open your invite link (it was sent to you by email, text, or your agent).
2. Enter your display name and choose a password.
3. Tap **Join workspace** — you're in.

If you already joined in a browser and now want to use the app: open the app → tap **Log in** → enter the same email and password you set when you joined.

## Having trouble?

**"Invite link not found" or "Token expired"**
Invite links expire after 7 days. Ask whoever sent it to generate a new one.

**TestFlight build expired**
TestFlight builds expire after 90 days. Check [testflight.apple.com](https://testflight.apple.com) for an updated build or reach out to the workspace owner.
